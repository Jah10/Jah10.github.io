Telecommunication & Industrial 

Our intech power systems are used to power communication equipment, such as cell towers, satellites, and base stations. We work with leading telecommunications companies to create innovative and reliable battery solutions that meet the demands of the industry. Our solutions are for a wide range of industrial applications. Our power systems can be used in heavy-duty equipment, such as forklifts, lift trucks, and material handling equipment. Our aim is to reduce energy costs and improve operational efficiency for our industrial clients by providing high-performance power systems that are both durable and reliable.

Agriculture 

Our intech power systems are also applicable in a variety of agricultural equipment, such as tractors, harvesters, and other heavy-duty machinery. Our aim is to improve the sustainability of agriculture by providing high-performance power systems that are both efficient and environmentally friendly. Our energy storage systems help farmers reduce their energy costs and improve the efficiency of their equipment, which in turn helps them maximize their crop yields, while opening the avenue for smart farming.

Automotive

With a focus on advancing energy storage, we provide innovative and reliable solutions for the automotive industry.
